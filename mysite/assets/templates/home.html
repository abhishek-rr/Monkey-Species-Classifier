Monkey Species Classifier

This monkey classifier is inspired by the Monkey species classification on Kaggle.
The Image classifier is trained on the resnet50 archtecture using Fast.ai library, and gets 99.2% accuracy.

The Images are classied into 10 species, for now. These species are: mantled howler, patas monkey, bald uakari, japanese macaque, pygmy marmoset, white headed capuchin, silvery marmoset, common squirrel monkey, black headed night monkey and nilgiri langur.
